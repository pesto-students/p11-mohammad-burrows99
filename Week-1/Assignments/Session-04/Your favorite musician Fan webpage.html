John Mayer

John Mayer is an American singer-songwriter and guitarist. He was born in Connecticut in 1977 and started playing the guitar at the age of 13. He became famous in the early 2000s with his hit singles "Your Body Is a Wonderland" and "Daughters". Mayer's music style is a mix of blues, rock, and pop, and he is known for his smooth vocals and excellent guitar skills.
